About Astral Flicks

Learn more about our passion for photography and our approach to capturing your story.

Creating Visual Stories That Last a Lifetime

At Astral Flicks, we believe that photography is more than just taking pictures—it's about preserving moments, emotions, and stories that you'll cherish for years to come.

Founded in 2015 by lead photographer Alex Morgan, our studio has grown into a team of dedicated professionals who share a passion for creating beautiful, authentic imagery.

We approach each project with fresh eyes and a commitment to understanding your unique vision. Whether it's a wedding, portrait session, or commercial project, we strive to create images that are not only technically excellent but also emotionally resonant.
